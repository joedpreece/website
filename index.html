About

I was born and raised in the vibrant town of Reading, where I developed my insatiable curiosity for science, art, and everything in between. Now, as an adopted Brummie, I live and work in the West Midlands, where I am constantly finding new projects to challenge myself.

Academia

I am a PhD Researcher at the Birmingham Centre for Railway Research and Education; a department at the University of Birmingham that drives innovation for railways around the world. My own research explores avenues of digitisation within the railway industry, with a specific focus on blockchain technology and the impact this will have on smart ticketing, logistics, and data decentralisation.

Outreach

I am one of the City Coordinators for the Pint of Science Festival 2020 in Birmingham, having volunteered and managed at the festival on two previous occasions. I am keen to present my own research and ideas to the general public, and am always seeking outreach opportunities to engage the next generation of scientists.
